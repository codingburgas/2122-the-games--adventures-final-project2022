About the game

"Kuker The Game" is a 2D game about a kuker with a stick
who is in a dungeon where he fights evil spirits using his stick.

On the kuker's sticks there are bells. The bells can
be bronze, silver or gold. The bronze one is the worst.
It has the shortest range and the worst damage.
The silver one is slightly better and the gold one is
the best of the three bells.

The dungeon will contain 6 to 8 rooms.
The kuker passes through a door to go a room.
He can go to any room he wants.

The Kuker can go to any room he wants but the game ends
when he kills the final boss, but to be able to kill him
he needs to kill a certain amount of the evil spirits,
wandering around the dungeon.
